What We Do

  1. Monitor and strengthen protections against security breaches and intrusion;
  2. Ensure computer infrastructure, applications, and all connected devices integrate without seams and gaps in security;
  3. Identify and assess security breaches immediately, and have tested, practised recovery action plans and drills ready to go.
  4. Ensure your business risk management program has sufficient Computer Security Breach insurance.

We provide a team of security specialists to work closely with you to:

  • Understand your security situation and risk management policy;
  • Identify security solutions that will strengthen and extend your current security protections;
  • Prioritize the solutions and select which ones need immediate attention;
  • Prepare a security improvement work plan tailored to your organization;
  • Build, test and deploy security solutions from our portfolio of proven security tools and techniques; and
  • Transfer security skills and knowledge so your security specialists can maintain high levels of security protection when the work plan is complete.
