Salient Security Services is here to help.

Our mission is to enable small and medium sized companies in the Niagara Region win their security battles. The enemy is aggressive and inventive; security attacks are constantly evolving. Maintaining adequate computer security is an ongoing struggle.

Computer services and data files are at great risk. The number of security breaches is skyrocketing. The cost to recover from a security event is now reported to range from $40,000 for a minor incident, to over ten million dollars for a severe ransom situation. Several large enterprises have even been forced into bankruptcy following a major cybersecurity attack.

Our portfolio of proven security solutions cover:

  • Protecting your computer operations and assets;
  • Detecting security events immediately when they occur;
  • Responding quickly to security incidents and breaches; and
  • Recovering as quickly as possible from the impact of a security event.

The internet is a dangerous place. Hackers, malware, ransomware, acts of fraud, phishing, identity theft and interception are rampant. Hundreds of small businesses are impacted every day. The question of whether you will be impacted is not one of “if”, but “when, and how much”. From being forced off-line, to being forced out of business, the risks are all too real. According to FBI data, the average ransomware demand is over $100,000. No business today can afford to ignore cyber security.
